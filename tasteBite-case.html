TasteBite Restaurant Website

TasteBite is a modern restaurant offering delicious continental and local cuisine made with fresh ingredients.

Role

Lead Developer

Duration

2 Months

Tools

The Challenge.

TasteBite needed a modern, engaging website that could effectively showcase their menu, location, and unique dining experience while providing an easy way for customers to make reservations and learn more about the restaurant.

The Solution.

Designed and developed a visually appealing and mobile-friendly restaurant website that showcases the brand, menu, and contact information in a clean and organized way.

  • Created a responsive design that adapts to different screen sizes and devices.
  • Implemented a user-friendly interface with easy navigation and clear calls-to-action.
  • Optimized the website for fast loading times and smooth performance.
